## Installation Instructions
### Using Installation Script (Windows)
1. Unzip the file.
2. Execute _install-Windows.bat_ or _install-Mac.sh_ depending on your OS.
3. A file browser will pop up. Select your Steam folder. (e.g. “_C:&#8203;\Program Files (x86)&#8203;\Steam_”)
4. Wait, and done!
5. You’ll find the new world at the top right corner of the screen when you enter the nexus.
    The Ice Palace will be unlocked after you see the ending.

### Installing Manually (Windows)
1. Unzip the file.
2. Copy _communityDLC_ folder into “Steam&#8203;\SteamApps&#8203;\common&#8203;\\_Recursed_” directory.
3. Copy _communityDLC.lua_ file into “Steam&#8203;\SteamApps&#8203;\common&#8203;\\_Recursed\data_” directory.
4. (Optional) For easy un/re-installation, backup _nexus.lua_ and other _nexus-\*.lua_ files in
    “Steam&#8203;\SteamApps&#8203;\common&#8203;\\_Recursed\data_” to somewhere safe.
5. Copy and overwrite *nexus.lua* and other *nexus-\*.lua* files into
    “Steam&#8203;\SteamApps&#8203;\common&#8203;\\_Recursed\data_” directory.
6. Done!
7. You’ll find the new world at the top right corner of the screen when you enter the nexus. The Ice Palace will be unlocked after you see the ending.
