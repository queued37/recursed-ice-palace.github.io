## Installation Instructions
### Using Installation Script (Windows)
1. Unzip the file.
2. Execute “install-Windows.bat” or “install-Mac.sh” depending on your OS.
3. A file browser will pop up. Select your Steam folder. (e.g. C:\Program Files (x86)\Steam\)
4. Wait, and done!
5. You’ll find the new world at the top right corner of the screen when you enter the nexus. The Ice Palace will be unlocked after you see the ending.

### Installing Manually
1. Unzip the file.
2. Copy “communityDLC” folder into Steam\SteamApps\common\Recursed directory.
3. Copy “communityDLC.lua” file into Steam\SteamApps\common\Recursed\data directory.
4. In preparation for uninstall, backup “nexus.lua” and other “nexus-??.lua” files in Steam\SteamApps\common\Recursed\data to somewhere safe.
5. Copy and overwrite “nexus.lua” and other “nexus-??.lua” files into Steam\SteamApps\common\Recursed\data directory.
6. Done!
7. You’ll find the new world at the top right corner of the screen when you enter the nexus. The Ice Palace will be unlocked after you see the ending.
